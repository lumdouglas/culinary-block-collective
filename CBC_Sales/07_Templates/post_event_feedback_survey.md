# Post-Event Feedback — Text/Email Templates

**Send:** Within 48 hours of delivery
**From:** Doug / CBC
**To:** Client's event contact

---

## Option A — Text Message (Preferred — Higher Response Rate)

```
Hi [First Name]! Doug from Culinary Block Collective. Hope the team enjoyed the [Cuisine] yesterday!

Quick 3 questions (reply with numbers or a quick note):

1. Food quality? (1-5, 5 = loved it)
2. Delivery on time and as expected? (Y/N)
3. Anything you'd change for next time?

Your feedback helps us get better. Thanks! 🙏
```
