### What to Expect on Event Day

**Before delivery:**
- We'll confirm everything with you the day before via text or email
- You'll receive a text from Doug when the food is en route

**At delivery (Tier 1 — Drop-Off):**
- Our team will deliver branded boxes, chafing dishes/trays, serving utensils, printed menu cards, and disposable plates/napkins to your specified location
- Every item is individually labeled for dietary needs (vegan, GF, nut-free, dairy-free)
- Setup and cleanup are handled by your team

**At delivery (Tier 2 — Attended Service):**
- Everything above, plus 1–2 CBC staff on-site for setup, buffet maintenance, and cleanup
- Proper linen and labeled stations included
- Our team handles everything — your people just show up and eat
