Corporate Catering

How much does corporate catering in San Jose cost?
Box lunches start at $15/person, buffets range from $20–$25/person. All prices include delivery, setup, and dietary labeling — no hidden fees.
What cuisines do you offer?
Sushi & Japanese, Chinese (Sichuan), Mexican Fusion, and Specialty/Seasonal. Mix and match for larger events.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Every item individually labeled for vegan, GF, nut-free, and dairy-free. Every order includes a full dietary breakdown sheet.
What areas do you serve?
San Jose, Palo Alto, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, and Santa Clara — within 20 miles of our San Jose kitchen.
Do you offer recurring catering contracts?
Yes — weekly or bi-weekly programs with locked pricing, a 90-day review clause, and a 5% standing discount. Proposal available on request.
