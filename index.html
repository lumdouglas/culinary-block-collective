FAQ

Questions About Our
Corporate Catering

How much does corporate catering in San Jose cost?
Culinary Block Collective offers corporate catering from $20–$30 per box, with a 50-box minimum. Standard boxes start at $20–$25 (Sichuan, Mexican fusion) and premium boxes at $25–$30 (sushi/Japanese). Full-service attended events are custom-quoted. All prices include delivery, dietary labeling, and a dedicated coordinator — no hidden fees.
What cuisines do you offer for corporate catering?
We partner with independent specialist chefs covering Sushi & Japanese, Chinese (Sichuan), Mexican Fusion, and Specialty/Seasonal global menus. You can choose one cuisine or mix multiple for larger events.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Yes — every single item is individually labeled for vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, and dairy-free options. Every order includes a full dietary breakdown sheet with labeled packaging, so no one has to guess what they're eating.
What areas do you serve for corporate catering?
We deliver across the Bay Area including San Jose, Palo Alto, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, and Santa Clara — within a 20-mile radius of our San Jose kitchen. Contact us for other locations.
How far in advance do I need to book?
We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for orders under 150 guests, and 5+ business days for larger events or custom mix-and-match orders. For recurring weekly accounts, a simple standing order is all you need — we handle the rest.
Do you offer discounts for recurring corporate catering orders?
Yes — we offer a 5% discount on all recurring weekly or bi-weekly catering contracts locked in for a minimum of 3 months. New clients also receive 10% off their first order. Slots are limited — contact us to check current availability.
Do you offer recurring weekly catering contracts for corporate accounts?
Yes — our recurring catering program is designed for HR and People Ops teams that want a reliable, consistent vendor. Programs include weekly or bi-weekly delivery cadence, locked per-box pricing for quarterly budget planning, a rotating multi-cuisine menu, and a 90-day review clause so you're never locked in long-term. A 3-month minimum applies. We provide a formal program proposal with cost projections on request.
Can you support our company's sustainability and ESG goals?
Yes — Culinary Block Collective uses BPI-certified compostable containers for all orders. We also offer a carbon offset add-on for recurring accounts. Upon request, we can provide a vendor sustainability summary formatted for internal ESG reporting or procurement review.
Why do you show prices when other caterers won't?
Most caterers hide pricing because it gives them negotiating leverage — if they know your budget first, they can quote to your ceiling. We publish prices upfront because you have a real budget to manage. You shouldn't need a sales call to know if we're in range. For large or complex events where a custom quote is needed, we'll have one back to you within 24 hours.
