FAQ

Questions About Our
Corporate Catering

How much does corporate catering in San Jose cost?
Box lunches start at $15/person, spreads range from $20–$25/person. All prices include delivery, setup, and dietary labeling — no hidden fees.
What cuisines do you offer?
Sushi & Japanese, Chinese, Mexican Fusion, and Specialty/Seasonal menus. Mix and match for larger events.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Every single item is individually labeled for vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, and dairy-free options. Every order includes a full dietary breakdown sheet.
What areas do you serve?
San Jose, Palo Alto, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, and Santa Clara — within 20 miles of our San Jose kitchen.
How far in advance do I need to book?
48 hours minimum for under 150 guests; 5+ business days for larger or mix-and-match events.
Do you offer recurring catering contracts?
Yes — weekly or bi-weekly programs with locked per-box pricing, rotating menus, and a 90-day review clause. Full proposal available on request.
