## 1. Campaign Overview

CBC is launching a 6-month brand awareness and sales growth campaign targeting tech startups and mid-size companies across the Bay Area. Goal: become the go-to corporate catering partner known for culinary diversity, reliability, and sustainability.

**Campaign Mission:**
> *"Introduce Bay Area tech teams to the most diverse, fresh, and reliable catering experience available — and convert awareness into recurring contracts."*

### Core Goals
- Build brand visibility across LinkedIn, Instagram, and local business networks
- Generate 100+ qualified leads from tech firms with 50–300 employees
- Convert 10–15 clients into paying catering contracts within 6 months
- Achieve an Average Order Value (AOV) of $550+ per event
- Secure at least 5 recurring weekly or monthly accounts by month 6

### Chef Lineup — Our Differentiator

| Cuisine | Specialty | Price Range |
|---|---|---|
| Chinese (Sichuan) | Wok dishes, dumplings, stir-fry, noodles | $20–$25/box |
| Sushi / Japanese | Rolls, sashimi, bento boxes, ramen | $25–$30/box |
| Mexican Fusion | Tacos, bowls, elote, tamales | $20–$25/box |
| American | BBQ, sandwiches, comfort classics, pub fare | $20–$25/box |
| Specialty / Fusion | Dietary-forward, seasonal, global fusion | $20–$30/box |

All menus: 100–300 guests. Vegan, GF, and allergen-labeled options across all cuisines.

## 3. Brand Awareness Strategy

*Primary focus for months 1–3. Build credibility, showcase chefs, stay top-of-mind before converting to buyers.*

### 3.1 LinkedIn (Primary Channel)
- Post 3x/week: chef spotlights, behind-the-scenes kitchen prep, client event recaps
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator ($79/month): target Office Managers & HR at Bay Area tech firms
- Connection campaigns: personalized invites with short hook about chef diversity
- 1 long-form article/month: e.g., "How to Choose a Caterer for Your All-Hands"
- 1 boosted post/month ($100–$200): targeting job titles within 30-mile radius

### 3.2 Instagram (Visual Showcase)
- Post 4x/week: food photography, Reels of chefs cooking, packaging, team events
- Reels (30–60 sec): specific dishes — sushi rolling, taco building, wok tossing
- Stories: daily polls ("What's your team's fave cuisine?"), Q&As, promo countdowns
- Hashtags: `#BayAreaCatering #TechLunchSJ #CulinaryBlock #CorporateCatering #SustainableEats`
- Partner with 2–3 Bay Area micro-influencers (5K–30K followers) for event coverage

### 3.3 Local Networking & Events
- Attend 2 Bay Area tech networking events/month — bring branded tasting cards
- Host a free tasting event at Culinary Block (invite 20 office managers, cap at 25 for scarcity)
- Flyers at shared office parks, WeWork, and startup hubs in SJ/Palo Alto
- Partner with 1 co-working space to cater a free "community lunch" for PR & word-of-mouth

### 3.4 Month 1 Content Calendar

| Week | Channel | Content | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | LinkedIn + IG | Launch post: "Meet Our Chefs" intro carousel | Awareness & follows |
| Week 2 | Instagram | Reel: Sushi chef rolling — "Your next team lunch" | Engagement & reach |
| Week 3 | LinkedIn | Outreach campaign: 50 personalized DMs to Office Managers | Lead gen (5–10 replies) |
| Week 4 | Email + IG | Email blast to collected contacts + IG promo for free tasting | List building + bookings |

*Use `/content-week` in the cbc-sales plugin to generate each week's posts automatically.*

## 4. Lead Generation & Sales Strategy

### 4.1 Lead Generation Channels
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** Target 200 Bay Area tech firms/month; connect with 50 decision-makers/week
- **Website Quote Form:** SEO-optimized landing page with instant quote tool
- **Mailchimp Email Campaigns:** Build list via free tasting sign-ups and LinkedIn; target 500 contacts by month 3
- **Referral Program:** 10% credit for any client who refers a paying customer
- **Google Business Profile:** Optimize with photos, menus, and review requests post-event

### 4.2 Sales Process Flow

| Step | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 — Awareness | LinkedIn post / DM / flyer → visitor lands on website |
| 2 — Interest | They browse menus, see chef profiles, request a quote |
| 3 — Consideration | 24-hour email response + free Zoom consultation offer |
| 4 — Decision | Custom quote sent; 10% first-order discount for new clients |
| 5 — Onboarding | Confirm order via HubSpot CRM; delivery checklist |
| 6 — Retention | Post-event NPS survey + upsell to recurring contract |

### 4.3 Promotional Offers

| Offer | Details | Scarcity Driver |
|---|---|---|
| 10% First-Order Discount | Applied to first order of 100+ guests | Valid for bookings by June 30, 2025 |
| Free Chef Tasting | In-person at Culinary Block for up to 4 guests | 10 slots/month |
| Recurring Account Bonus | 5% monthly discount for weekly/bi-weekly contracts | 3-month minimum |

### 4.4 Upselling
- Juice & Boba Bundles: $12/pack — at checkout and in follow-up emails
- Premium Packaging: compostable containers + branded napkins, +$3/person
- Chef's Table Upgrade: on-site chef for VIP events, +$150/event
- Organic Ingredient Tier: +15% for premium organic sourcing

### Risk & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Low lead conversion (<5%) | Increase LinkedIn ad spend to $200/month; A/B test subject lines |
| Delivery issues | Chef Delivery Protocol (see `docs/operations.md`) enforced at onboarding; pre-event day-before confirmation required; Doug available as backup delivery at negotiated per-mile rate |
| High food costs | Bulk-purchase containers; renegotiate farm suppliers quarterly |
| Low social engagement | A/B test post types (Reels vs. carousels); 2 micro-influencer posts/month |
