## How to Use This Document

1. Personalize all fields in `[BRACKETS]` before sending
2. Follow the send timing noted beside each email title
3. Track opens and replies in HubSpot CRM — log every interaction
4. Attach the Culinary Block one-pager PDF to **Email 1** and **Email 3**
5. If a prospect replies at any point — move them to the Quote Process immediately

## Email 4: The Re-Engagement Email
*Send: 3 weeks after Email 3 (for cold/quiet leads)*

**To:** [Same contact]

**Subject:** Closing the loop — Culinary Block

---

Hi [First Name],

I know inboxes get busy — totally get it.

I'm closing out my outreach to [Company] unless now is actually a better time to connect.

Quick version of who we are:
- Specialized chefs: sushi, Chinese, Mexican fusion, American, specialty
- Corporate catering for 50–300 people, $20–$30/box
- On-time delivery, labeled packaging, transparent pricing
- Free chef tasting available for corporate accounts (10 slots/month)

If catering ever comes up — even months from now — I'd love to be on your radar.

Reply with **'Not now'** and I'll reach back out in Q3.
Reply with **'Tell me more'** and I'll send menus and a quote right away.

Either way, thanks for your time.

[Your Name]
Culinary Block Collective
[Phone] | culinaryblockcollective.com

> 💡 **Tip:** Keep this email short and pressure-free. The goal is a 'yes' or a 'not yet' — both are wins.

I hope the event went smoothly — and that the team enjoyed the food!

If it went well, we'd be grateful if you'd leave us a quick Google review: [Google Review Link]

**RECURRING CATERING ACCOUNTS** — 5% discount for weekly or bi-weekly bookings. Great for all-hands lunches or standing team meals.

**JUICE & BOBA ADD-ON** — $12/pack. Popular addition for afternoon meetings.

**REFERRAL PROGRAM** — Refer another company and earn 10% credit on your next order.

Thanks again, [First Name] — it was a pleasure working with you.

[Your Name]
Culinary Block Collective | culinaryblockcollective.com
