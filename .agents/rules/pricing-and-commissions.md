## Commission Structure (Internal Only)

### Chef John — Five Course Provision

- **CBC: 12% / Chef: 88%**
- Performance escalator: adjusts to **15% CBC / 85% chef** if collected sales exceed **$15K in any rolling 30-day period**
- Chef John absorbs credit card processing fees (~3%) — deducted from his 88% share before CBC pays out
- Late delivery credit (10% off next order) deducted from Chef John's payout, not CBC's share
- CBC is sales-only — Chef handles all delivery, staging, labeling, client fulfillment

| Price Point | Chef Gets | CBC Gets |
|---|---|---|
| $34/person (Standard) | $29.92 | $4.08 |
| $40/person (Premium) | $35.20 | $4.80 |

## Quote Building Rules

1. Per-person rate × guest count = food subtotal
2. Add applicable add-ons
3. Apply 10% first-order discount if new client (100+ guests only; valid through 2026-09-30)
4. Add **$150 Coordination & Service Fee** as a separate line item
5. Sales tax collected by CBC on top of total — chefs are not taxed on their payout
6. Quote valid for **14 days**
7. Turnaround: **24 hours max**

## Deposit Rules

- **25% of total order value** required to confirm booking
- Non-negotiable — no deposit = no confirmed date
- Applied to final invoice at time of payment

## Recurring Account Discount

- **NOT active at launch**
- Planned: 5% discount for standing weekly/bi-weekly orders
- Introduce only after chef agreements are signed and orders are flowing
- When active: discount applied to client total first, then per-chef split applies to the discounted amount (both CBC and chef absorb proportionally)
