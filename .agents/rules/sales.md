# CBC Sales Rules

---

## Target Personas

### Persona A — Sarah (Office Manager / Workplace Ops)
- Title: Office Manager, Executive Assistant, Workplace Ops Coordinator
- Company: Series B–C tech firms, 75–200 employees
- Pain: reliability failures, day-of surprises, wasted morning managing the caterer
- Lead angle: on-time guarantee, dietary accuracy, transparent pricing

### Persona B — Marcus (HR Director / Head of People)
- Title: HR Director, Head of People, VP People Ops
- Company: Series C–D or post-IPO tech firms, 150–300 employees
- Pain: food culture not reflecting team diversity, ESG gaps, eNPS drag
- Lead angle: specialist chefs, diverse cuisines, sustainability, culture-forward program

**Geographic focus:** Palo Alto, San Jose, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Santa Clara

## Inbound Discovery Script (Ask All 5)

1. "What's the occasion — team lunch, client dinner, all-hands, or something else?"
2. "How many people are you expecting?"
3. "Do you have a date in mind, or are you still planning?"
4. "Any dietary restrictions I should know about — vegan, gluten-free, allergies?"
5. "What's your rough budget per person — standard spread or premium with upgraded proteins?"

## Outbound Cold Call Opening

> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Culinary Block Collective. I know this is out of the blue — do you have 45 seconds? ... We're a corporate catering kitchen in San Jose — sushi, Chinese, Mexican fusion. We work with tech teams, usually for team lunches and product launches. Do you ever use outside caterers for your office events?"

## Quote Follow-Up Script

> "Just checking on the quote I sent — happy to adjust the menu or hold a delivery slot."

---

## First-Order Offer

- **10% discount** on first order of 100+ guests
- Valid for bookings by **September 30, 2026**
- Free Chef Tasting available (10 slots/month, in-person at Culinary Block, up to 4 guests)

---

## Recurring Contract Program

- Minimum 3-month commitment
- Weekly or bi-weekly standing orders
- Fixed per-head pricing (locked for budget forecasting)
- Rotating multi-cuisine menu
- 90-day review clause (no long-term lock-in)
- Pitch after every successful first order
- **5% recurring discount: NOT active at launch** — introduce only after chef agreements are signed

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Review HubSpot pipeline (Monday morning)
- [ ] Send 25 new outreach messages (5/day, Mon–Fri)
- [ ] Follow up on Email 1 opens with no reply (Tuesday)
- [ ] Post LinkedIn content (2–3x/week)
- [ ] Post Instagram Reel or Story (3–4x/week)
- [ ] Update Google Business Profile (1x/week)
- [ ] Send quotes to all inbound requests same day
- [ ] Call/text any quote 48+ hours old with no response (Wednesday)
- [ ] Ask most recent client for Google review (within 48 hours of delivery)
- [ ] Ask 1 happy client for a referral
