# CBC Brand Voice & Messaging Rules

---

## Brand Positioning

- Professional but warm, community-oriented, chef-forward
- Lead with **outcomes** (reliability, dietary accuracy, team satisfaction) — not features
- Emphasize specialist chefs, not a generic catering company

## Core Trust Badges (Use in All Marketing)

> "On-Time Delivery Guaranteed · Every Item Dietary-Labeled · Compostable Tableware Included · Independent Specialist Chefs"

---

## Language Rules

### Always Say

| Instead of... | Say... |
|---|---|
| "in-house chefs" | "independent specialist chefs" |
| "catering menu" | "Chef's Spread" |
| "food delivery" | "Chef's Spread delivery" |
| "our kitchen" | "our commissary" or "Culinary Block" |

### Never Say

- "in-house chefs" — chefs are independent operators, not employees
- Anything that implies a fixed chef count (roster is dynamic)
- Commission rates or payout percentages
- Which chef delivers and which CBC delivers (operational backend is not client-facing)
- "No hidden fees" followed by a surprise $150 fee — the fee is disclosed upfront on every quote

## Persona-Specific Tone

### Persona A — Sarah (Office Manager / Workplace Ops)

Pain: reliability failures, day-of surprises, wasted morning managing the caterer
Lead with: "Catering that doesn't require babysitting the morning of"
Tone: practical, direct, peer-to-peer
Value message: on-time guarantee, dietary labeling, transparent pricing

**Email subject lines (ranked):**
1. "Catering that doesn't require babysitting the morning of" ← recommended
2. "The Bay Area caterer your team will actually request by name"
3. "Quick question about catering at [Company]"
4. "[Company] team lunch — one thing most caterers won't put in writing"

### Persona B — Marcus (HR Director / Head of People)

Pain: food culture not reflecting team diversity, ESG gaps, eNPS drag from bad food
Lead with: "Your catering vendor is a culture statement — does yours reflect your team?"
Tone: strategic, culture-forward, ROI-aware
Value message: diverse specialist chefs, sustainability options, recurring program

**Email subject lines (ranked):**
1. "Your catering vendor is a culture statement — does yours reflect your team?" ← recommended
2. "The People Ops angle on corporate catering most vendors miss"
3. "[Company]'s food program — quick question"
4. "ESG-reportable catering + eNPS ROI — worth 15 minutes?"
