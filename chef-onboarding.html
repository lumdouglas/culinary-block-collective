📋 Cancellation Policy — Please Read
As a CBC chef partner, a confirmed order is a commitment. Here's what happens if either side needs to cancel:

If you need to cancel:
• 7+ days before the event — No penalty. CBC will find a replacement. Please give as much notice as possible so the client isn't impacted.
• Within 7 days of a confirmed order — A $250 cancellation fee is owed to CBC. Why: at this point CBC has confirmed to the client, allocated service kit materials, and may have turned down other bookings for that date. The fee covers those real costs.

If the client cancels:
• 7+ days out — Client gets a full deposit refund. No impact to you — CBC rebooks the slot.
• Within 7 days — Client forfeits the 25% deposit (or CBC applies it as credit to a future order). You don't receive a payment for that order, but you also owe nothing.
• Within 72 hours — Client is charged 50% of the order total. CBC keeps 25% (the deposit). You receive 25% of the order value to cover your food and ingredient costs.

These policies exist to protect you as much as Doug. Your time and ingredients have real value. Questions? Ask Doug before you sign.
