Michelin-Trained · Premium Tier

Chef John trained at Quince (★★★), Providence (★★), and Gwen (★) before spending two years under Rick Bayless in Chicago. These prices reflect chef-driven menus with fine-dining technique — not a catering operation running the same playbook for every client.

🌱

Dietary accommodations across all four concepts

Vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, and dairy-free options available across all cuisines. Mention your team's needs at booking.

🏷️

Every item individually labeled at delivery

Dietary tags, allergen callouts, and consume-by times on every tray — food safety included by default.

🌱

Dietary Labeling — Every Order, Every Item

CBC prints and applies dietary labels at delivery: Vegan, Gluten-Free, Contains Nuts, Contains Dairy, and consume-by times for food safety. Share any team dietary restrictions at booking so we can flag them clearly on-site.
